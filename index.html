My name is Stacy Festger, and I have been crocheting for 20 years. I am always crocheting, be it for friends, family, clients, or even for fun. I make custom designs and pull designs from the web to better my craft. Feel free to contact me if you are interested in having something made for you or if you are interested in crocheting advice or suggestions!
